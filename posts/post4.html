Once your blog starts attracting readers, there are several ways to earn income without overwhelming your visitors:

  • Affiliate marketing: Join programs like Amazon Associates or Udemy. Recommend products or courses relevant to your niche and earn a commission whenever someone makes a purchase through your link. This method has low start‑up costs and can scale well【22†L266-L274】.
  • Display ads: Use networks like Google AdSense or Ezoic to place ads on your site. As your traffic grows, ad revenue can provide a steady stream of passive income【22†L237-L245】. Avoid placing too many ads early on; focus on user experience first.
  • Sell digital products: Package your own knowledge into eBooks, guides or online courses. Digital products have high margins because you create them once and sell them repeatedly【11†L61-L69】. For example, you could compile your AI productivity tips into a PDF and sell it via Gumroad.
  • Sponsored posts: Once you have a loyal audience, companies may pay you to review or mention their products. Always disclose sponsorships and only promote products you genuinely believe in.

Regardless of which methods you choose, always prioritise providing value to your readers. Monetisation should enhance the experience, not detract from it.
